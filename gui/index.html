Did you know that people in Europe are more likely to be Vitamin D deficient?

The British Skin Foundation, desrcibing a study by the Universiy of Macnhester say: "In simple terms, they have found that - for lighter skin types - daily sunlight exposure for 10-15 minutes between April and September provides sufficient year-round vitamin D while also minimising the risks of sunburn and skin cancer. For darker skin types, 25-40 minutes is recommended."

Source: British Skin Foundation - Sunlight and Vitamin D
